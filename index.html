Hi. We are Marcos Lodicakes.

And this is our project, Analysis of Tweets on Marcos Corruption Misinformation, it mainly scans through the tweets made by users in spreading mis/disinformation regarding Marcos during a particular period of time. This mainly uses data science to analyze various tweets made by different users and determine its effect on the country.

Research Question

Were the mis/disinformed tweets made to bring the Marcos name to a better light at relevant times/dates/events with respect to the 2022 elections?

Hypothesis:

The tweets were made strategically at dates where election-related news/events were happening.

Null Hypothesis

The tweets were made at random times where the date of tweeting is irrelevant to the election.

Action Plan:

Analyze the dates of the tweets and the election-related news/events that happened during the time of tweeting.

Pre-processing

First: Remove all columns with NaN data.

Second: Create a column for Engagements which contains the sum of likes, retweets, quote retweets and replies per tweet.

Third: Translate the date posted string into date time format.

Fourth: Convert emojis into words and turn all letters into lowercase.

Fifth: Create a new data frame with columns that are needed for analysis (Time Series Analysis) which are Tweets, Date posted and Engagements.

Sixth: Translate the tweets into English and remove all the stop words.
